## What is a Hero Section?

A Hero Section is the large introductory section displayed
at the top of a webpage.

Its purpose is to immediately communicate:

• What the website is.

• Why the visitor is here.

• What action they should take next.

A Hero Section often contains:

• A large image.

• A heading.

• A short description.

• A Call-To-Action (CTA) button.
