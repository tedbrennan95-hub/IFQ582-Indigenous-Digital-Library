Acknowledgement of Country

We acknowledge the Traditional Owners of the lands on which this digital library is accessed and operated.

We pay our respects to Elders past and present, and recognise their continuing connection to Country, culture, language and community.

This application has been developed as part of an educational project and demonstrates how technology can support respectful management of Indigenous cultural collections through community-led access and review.

How the Library Works

The library supports public discovery, culturally appropriate access requests, and authorised community review.

Discover Collections

Browse digitised cultural collection items and view available public metadata.

Request Access

Community Review
